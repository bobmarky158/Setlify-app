What is BlackHole?

BlackHole Is An Awesome Music Player App Made With Flutter


Do You Like Online Music Streaming, But Tired Of Low Quality Or Paid Subscriptions? Would You Like Feature-rich And User Friendly App For That?


Blackhole Is Created With The Purpose Of High Quality Music Streaming Experience On Your Smartphone Without Annoying Ads And Very Minimal Interface


The Application Is Open Source And You Can Check On It At GitHub
